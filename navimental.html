Navimental

Design + Development for a Startup Supporting Parents

Overview

Navimental is a startup helping parents of children struggling with cognitive and behavioral challenges connect with professional support, manage journal entries, visualize progress, and access additional aid to better navigate their journey.

My Contributions

  • Designed intuitive journal entry flows for parents to log changes in their child’s behavior and progress.
  • Developed the frontend interface with accessibility and ease of use as priorities.
  • Created data visualizations to track progress over time.
  • Collaborated with the startup team to iterate quickly on UX/UI decisions.

Highlights

I combined UX design and frontend development to help parents gain confidence and clarity in supporting their children. The platform is built with compassion and usability at its core.
